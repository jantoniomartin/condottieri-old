{% trans "About the game" %}

{% blocktrans %}What happens when the time limit is reached?{% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %}It depends on your current status. If you have completed your phase, nothing happens. However, if you haven't, the following will happen:{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}If you have to place new units, you can't place them this turn.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}If you have to disband n units, your latest n units will be disbanded.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}If you have to write your orders, all your units will get a 'Hold' order.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}If you have to retreat any units, these units will be disbanded.{% endblocktrans %}
